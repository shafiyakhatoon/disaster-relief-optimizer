Key Algorithmic Insights
Why Greedy Can Fail Greedy picks the highest impact-ratio item first. It can fill capacity with one large item, missing combinations of smaller items that collectively yield higher total impact.
Why DP Always Wins The 0/1 Knapsack DP evaluates every possible subset by building a table of optimal solutions for each sub-capacity, guaranteeing the global maximum impact.
Impact Formula Impact = (Urgency × Population) / Distance — balances crisis severity, scale of affected population, and delivery logistics difficulty.
Real-World Trade-off Use Greedy when speed is critical (active crisis response). Use DP in planning phases when time permits exhaustive computation.
